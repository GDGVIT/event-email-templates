What it is

DevJams 2019

DevJams, a college level hackathon, aims to bring out the best of the talents in the fields of Coding, Developing and Designing. DevJams acts as a platform for the developers to showcase and present their innovative ideas through the hackathon. DevJams, a one-stop spot where excellent speakers and experts from the industry, mentor and motivate enthusiastic participants and share their past experiences with them.

Where is this

At Vellore Institute of Technology

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore is one of the top-rated engineering colleges in India. The University works on ideas and executes tasks of different domains under Student Clubs and Chapters. VIT University has more than 30,000 students enrolled in several streams.

Event Flow

Networking

Developers will be discussing their project ideas and will be interacting with industry-leading experts to improve their project. Experts, Speakers and Guests will get a chance to interact with some of the best 'emerging' innovators of the country. Who knows what it can lead to? The next big revolution?

Hackathon

The 36 - hour long hackathon will be spread across in various stages, filtering out the best teams that will compete in the final round. Participants will be encouraged to solve real-life problems by implementing the ideas given in the tracks, under the constant mentorship of industrial experts.

Incubation

If your company finds a project which might be deserving enough, you can be a part of the incubator program. It would be a great chance for the participants to get a kickstart on their projects and for you to know some of the most creative minds for you to incubate!
