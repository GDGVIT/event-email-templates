What is it - About BuildYou.com


BuildYou.com is a unique opportunity for all the budding developers out there to code along, and learn how to build and customize their own fully-working personal portfolio website from scratch! It would not only help them to kick-start their journey in web development, but also come up with a complete functional personal and customized portfolio of their own, built by themselves.

It would be a three hour technical workshop which introduces the student participants to the basics of web development stack such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript and promises to give them insight and gain a newly developed skill-set and a live hosted self-portfolio website to show off!







Who’s doing it - About DSC VIT


One of the most sought – after technical chapters in VIT Vellore is Developer Student Clubs VIT, powered by Google Developers, a non-profit community that aims to inspire young and intelligent minds in the field of technology and provides a platform to learn, develop and innovate. An organisation that believes in empowering enthusiasts in developing in various technical domains and building solutions for real-world problems by working alongside a well-established design and management team.
