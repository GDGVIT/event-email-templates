What is it?
DevJams is one of a kind hackathon that goes forth with the aim of encouraging healthy competition in order to incubate futuristic ideas. This hackathon focuses on providing students and upcoming entrepreneurs with a platform to develop, compete, network and present their ideas to solve real-world problems. We have been successfully conducting DevJams (previously known as Devfest) for 5 years now. DevJams’21 will be the sixth edition through which we hope to bring innovative minds from all around the country under one roof.
 
 
Where is it?
This year, with the current pandemic in mind, we have moved the entire event to be on an online platform. We will also see the inclusion of Mentors who will guide every team in implementing their projects, paving the way for a more professional approach to the idea throughout multiple phases of the hackathon.
Who is doing it?
One of the most sought – after technical chapters in VIT Vellore is Developer Student Clubs VIT, a non-profit community that aims to inspire young and intelligent minds in the field of technology and provides a platform to learn, develop and innovate.
